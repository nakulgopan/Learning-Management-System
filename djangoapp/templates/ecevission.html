Vision and Mission

Vision
 To be a Centre of Excellence in Electronic and Communication Engineering by imparting quality education and research that nourishes the students to be leaders and innovators to serve for the betterment of society
Mission
 M1. To equip the students with advanced knowledge in Electronic and Communication Engineering, versatility and an inquisitive attitude, which in tum helps them to explore innovative solu tions for the betterment of society.
M2. To provide undergraduate research experiences to address the challenges in Electronic and Communication Engineering and disseminate this knowledge at national and international venues.
M3. To have a constant updating in emerging technologies in the field of Electronic and Communication Engineering through industry institute interaction .
