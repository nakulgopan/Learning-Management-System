The Governing Body of our aided college is the apex administrative authority responsible for the overall management and strategic planning of the institution.The institution under controlled government of Kerala under department of Intelligent, Since 1993. Established under the guidelines of the University Grants Commission (UGC) and the affiliating KS technical University, the Governing Body consists of representatives from the management, teaching faculty, non-teaching staff, university nominees, and government officials. It undertakes the crucial task of ensuring that the college functions in accordance with the rules and regulations laid down by the government and regulatory bodies. The Governing Body oversees budget allocations, infrastructure development, recruitment of staff, and policy decisions that enhance the academic and administrative performance of the institution. Through its committed leadership and transparent governance, the Governing Body plays a vital role in maintaining the standards of quality education and promoting the vision and mission of the college.Chairman of the institution is the Hon'able minister D.B.Das, Vice Chairman Intelligent Department Head Dr.P.K.Ramdas ,Principal of the institution is Dr.Priyadarshini.N.
