Publications

At the College of Engineering Muttathara, faculty research and academic contributions form a core pillar of our institutional excellence. Our educators not only deliver quality education but also engage actively in cutting-edge research across multiple domains of science, engineering, and technology. Over the years, our faculty members have published numerous papers in reputed national and international journals, presented their findings in global conferences, and contributed chapters to academic books. These publications reflect the intellectual depth, innovation, and interdisciplinary focus of our academic staff. The Department of Civil Engineering, in particular, has produced impactful research in areas such as sustainable construction practices, urban planning, structural engineering, and water resource management.
