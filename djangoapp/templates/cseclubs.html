Clubs and Department Commities


1. Each semester has a class committee constituted by Class tutor, faculty handling different courses and 2 student representatives. Meetings are held 3 times per semester, 2 after each series and an introductory meeting at the beginning of each sem.
2. Scrutiny committee was formed for scrutinizing the question papers of 8 th sem exam KTU online exam, as the final sem KTU question paper was prepared from within the department. Each course scrutiny committee was constituted by HOD and a senior faculty member.
3. Meetings for analysing the result of each sem would also be conducted by the HOD after the publication of results by KTU.
